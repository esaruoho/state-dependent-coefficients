Pedagogical instrument built from the poster "State-Dependent Coefficients in Electrical-Engineering Pedagogy" — the interactive form of VR Paper 0's considered-constant audit. Functional forms are physically-motivated illustrations (core saturation with material-set knee sharpness and Curie roll-off, Class-II ceramic bias roll-off, copper temperature coefficient, Villari stress effect), not a SPICE-grade device model. The point is the shape of the coupling and the fact that the parametric second term is non-zero exactly when a coefficient depends on its own state variable. No over-unity is implied or claimed; the parametric section is Manley–Rowe bounded.
